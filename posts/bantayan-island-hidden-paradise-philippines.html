Bantayan Island: A Hidden Paradise in the Philippines

December 27, 2018
Back to Blog Destination Guide

Located off the coast of Cebu, Bantayan Island is a true hidden paradise in the Philippines. With its white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical forests, Bantayan Island is the perfect destination for anyone looking for a relaxed and rejuvenating getaway. Here's a guide to help you explore the beauty of Bantayan Island.

https://youtu.be/H3H60rcNRzE
  1. Beaches: The main draw of Bantayan Island is its stunning beaches, including Sugar Beach, Ogtong Cave Beach, and Paradise Beach. These white-sand beaches are surrounded by crystal-clear waters and offer the perfect setting for sunbathing, swimming, and snorkeling.
  2. Ogtong Cave: This unique natural feature is located on Ogtong Cave Beach and offers visitors a chance to swim and explore the interior of a limestone cave. The cool and refreshing waters of the cave make it a popular spot for swimming and snorkeling.
  3. St. Peter and Paul Church: This historic church is located in the town of Bantayan and is known for its stunning Spanish-colonial architecture. The church is an important part of the island's history and is well worth a visit for anyone interested in local culture and history.
  4. Mangrove Forest: Bantayan Island is home to a lush mangrove forest that offers a unique opportunity to explore the local flora and fauna. Take a guided tour of the mangrove forest to see exotic birds, reptiles, and other wildlife, and learn about the important role of mangroves in the local ecosystem.
  5. Island Hopping: Bantayan Island is surrounded by several smaller islands, including Virgin Island and Hilantagaan Island. Take a guided island-hopping tour to explore the beauty of these lesser-known destinations and enjoy the stunning scenery and crystal-clear waters.

Bantayan Island is a true hidden gem in the Philippines that offers a relaxed and rejuvenating escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. So pack your bags, grab your sunscreen, and get ready to discover the beauty of this hidden paradise in the Philippines.
