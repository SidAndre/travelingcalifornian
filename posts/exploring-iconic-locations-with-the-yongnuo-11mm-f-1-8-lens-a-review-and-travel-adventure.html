Exploring Iconic Locations with the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 Lens: A Review and Travel Adventure

June 03, 2024
Back to Blog Gear Review

Welcome back, fellow travelers and photography enthusiasts! Today, we're taking the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens on an exciting journey through some of the most stunning locations. If you're a travel photographer or a content creator looking for a versatile and affordable wide-angle lens, this review and travel experience will be invaluable. Watch the full video here.

Introduction to the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 Lens

In this video, we explore the capabilities of the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens as we capture the beauty of Millerton Lake, Sequoia National Park, and the vibrant city of Las Vegas. This ultra-wide-angle lens is perfect for capturing expansive landscapes and dynamic cityscapes, making it an ideal choice for travel photography enthusiasts.

Key Features of the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 Lens

Before diving into our adventure, let's highlight some of the key features of the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens:

  • Ultra-Wide Angle: The 11mm focal length provides a broad field of view, perfect for capturing vast landscapes and dramatic perspectives.
  • Bright Aperture: With a maximum aperture of f/1.8, this lens excels in low-light conditions and offers beautiful background blur for creative shots.
  • Compact and Lightweight: Easy to carry on your travels, this lens won't weigh you down, making it ideal for on-the-go photography.

Millerton Lake Adventure

Our journey begins at Millerton Lake, where the serene waters and surrounding landscapes provide an excellent opportunity to test the wide-angle capabilities of the lens. The Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 captures the expansive views of the lake, the reflections on the water, and the vibrant colors of the sunset with stunning clarity and detail.

Exploring Sequoia National Park

Next, we head to Sequoia National Park, home to some of the tallest trees in the world. The towering sequoias and the lush forest create a perfect setting for wide-angle photography. The lens performs admirably in the dappled light of the forest, capturing the immense scale and grandeur of the sequoias while handling the varying lighting conditions with ease.

Capturing the Vibrancy of Las Vegas

Our final destination is the bustling city of Las Vegas. The dynamic street scenes, vibrant lights, and iconic landmarks offer diverse photography opportunities. The Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens excels in capturing the energy and excitement of the city, from the bright lights of the Strip to the detailed architecture of the casinos. Even in low light, the lens produces sharp and vivid images, making it a great companion for nighttime photography.

Pros and Cons

After our extensive field test, here are some pros and cons of the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens:

Pros:

  • Excellent wide-angle coverage.
  • Great performance in low light.
  • Lightweight and easy to carry.
  • Affordable price point.

Cons:

  • Some distortion at the edges (common with ultra-wide lenses).
  • Manual focus only, which might be challenging for some users.

Conclusion

The Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 lens proves to be a fantastic companion for travel photography, offering exceptional value for its price. Whether you're capturing the serene beauty of Millerton Lake, the majestic trees of Sequoia National Park, or the vibrant energy of Las Vegas, this lens delivers impressive results.
