Monterey Bay: California's Coastal Wonders Uncovered

February 06, 2023
Back to Blog Destination Guide

Monterey Bay, located on California's central coast, is a true gem that offers breathtaking natural beauty, rich history, and a range of recreational activities for visitors. From world-class surf spots to the famous Monterey Bay Aquarium, there's something for everyone in this stunning area. Here's a guide to help you discover the beauty of Monterey Bay.

Advertisement728×90300×250
https://youtu.be/75EiZCP4BaY
  1. Monterey Bay Aquarium: This world-renowned facility is a must-visit for anyone interested in marine life. With a focus on conservation and sustainability, the aquarium features over 35,000 animals and plants in its 200+ exhibits, including jellyfish, sea otters, and many species of fish and invertebrates.
  2. Cannery Row: This historic street, once home to sardine canneries, is now a popular destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment. Walk along the harbor and enjoy the fresh sea air and the lively atmosphere of this charming area.
  3. Pacific Grove: This beautiful coastal town is known for its Victorian-style homes, scenic trails, and breathtaking views of the ocean. Take a stroll down Ocean View Boulevard to see some of the area's finest homes, and be sure to visit the Point Pinos Lighthouse for panoramic views of the bay.
  4. Big Sur: Just south of Monterey Bay is Big Sur, a rugged stretch of coastline with towering cliffs, rolling hills, and breathtaking vistas. Take a drive along the famous Pacific Coast Highway to see some of the most spectacular scenery in the world, and be sure to stop at one of the many scenic overlooks along the way.
  5. Surfing: Monterey Bay is known for its world-class surf spots, and the area is home to several surf schools where visitors can take lessons or rent equipment. Whether you're an experienced surfer or a beginner, you're sure to enjoy the waves of this iconic surf destination.

Whether you're interested in exploring the ocean, learning about marine life, or simply taking in the breathtaking scenery, Monterey Bay is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting California's Treasure Coast. So pack your bags, grab your sunscreen, and get ready to discover the beauty of this amazing area.
