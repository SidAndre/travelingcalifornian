The Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 is an ultra-wide lens that opens up a whole new world of creative possibilities for travel videographers and photographers.

## Las Vegas — Wide Angle Wonder

The Strip looks incredible through an 11mm lens. The dramatic perspective makes those towering casinos look even more impressive, and the wide field of view means you can capture the full scene without backing up into traffic.

## Sequoia — Nature at Its Best

Giant sequoias are literally too big for most lenses. The 11mm lets you capture the full height of these ancient trees in a single frame — something that's impossible with a standard kit lens.

## Worth the Investment?

At its price point, the Yongnuo 11mm f/1.8 is incredible value. It's become one of our most-used lenses on family travel adventures.
